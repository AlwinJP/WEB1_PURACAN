"A Hand-Made Wallet"

An ill child was brought to a hospital. after her treatment, the mother showed the doctor a hand-made wallet and said:

"There are things we can't pay. I hope ypu take this wallet I made for you."

The doctor said:

"I don't practice for sentimental reasons. Pay me P10000. Then she replied:"

"You are a very good doctor but I wish you were a better person."
